Process

Clear steps from first call to handover

A simple, transparent process that respects your time, budget and site conditions.

1. Initial discussion

Understand your space, requirements, timelines and budget over call or WhatsApp, with basic drawings if available.

2. Site visit

Visit the site to take measurements, study services and note constraints that will impact furniture or POP work.

3. Drawings & 3D

Prepare 3D views or working drawings and refine them with you so everything is clear before work begins.

4. Quotation & schedule

Share scope-wise quotation with materials, finishes and tentative schedule for execution.

5. Execution & supervision

Start site execution with regular supervision, quality checks and coordination with other agencies.

6. Finishing & handover

Final fittings, cleaning and handover, with basic after-service support as mutually agreed.
